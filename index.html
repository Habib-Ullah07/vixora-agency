Common Questions

Before you book a call

Do you only work with software clients, or only creative clients?

Neither exclusively — that's the point of Vixora. We take software-only projects, creative-only projects, and combined engagements where the same team handles both.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Vixora works remotely with clients across the UK, GCC and other international markets, alongside local Pakistani businesses, using async updates and scheduled calls across time zones.

How long does a typical project take?

A single video ad or creative package usually takes 1–2 weeks. Software builds vary by scope — a focused POS or business app typically runs 4–10 weeks. We confirm an exact timeline before any work starts.

What do you need from me to get started?

For creative work: your product details, target audience and any existing brand assets. For software: a description of the workflow you want to digitize and access to relevant data or systems if any exist.

How is pricing structured?

Creative projects are typically quoted per deliverable; software projects are quoted per scope after a short discovery call. We share a fixed quote before starting — no open-ended hourly billing surprises.
